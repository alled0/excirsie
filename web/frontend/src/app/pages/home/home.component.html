Tips for best results

  • ✓ Film from a side or front angle at roughly shoulder height.
  • ✓ Make sure your full body is in frame throughout the set.
  • ✓ Good lighting helps — avoid filming with a bright window behind you.
  • ✓ Keep the camera still; shaky footage reduces accuracy.
  • ✕ Avoid very long clips — a single set of 8–15 reps works best.
